Improve customer experience

Cannyi brings retail stores to life with interactive savings.

Instead of just mailing promotional savings to your customers, now they can be offered in stores. Cannyi offers an additional channel to bringing customers back in. Using image recognition, when targeted markers are scanned by a customer an augmented promotional savings will be shown.

Improve placement of retail products

Cannyi can be used to help improve diagram model to increase sales

Targeted Markers can be strategically placed in areas of the store that lack foot traffic. Promotions can be in any location or on any shelf to ensure customer gets visibility of a specific area of a store.

Realtime Messaging service

Notify customers when a new Cannyi promotion is added.

Providing realtime messaging services Cannyi can alert your customers when new promotional savings are added in stores. Instead of waiting for customers to see promotions online or for promotions to arrive in the mail, customers get realtime updates.
